## What is the Pool?

The **Piscine** is an intensive 26‑day training period at 42.  
It is designed to test your ability to learn independently and work under pressure.

 No traditional teachers  
 Peer‑to‑peer evaluation  
 Self‑learning through research 

 Focus areas:  
   **C programming**  
   **Algorithmic thinking**  
   **Problem‑solving**
